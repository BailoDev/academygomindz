Welcome To Gomindz Academy

About Us

At Gomindz Academy we recognize the immese potential that lies within the vibrant youths of the Gambia.

01

Mission

To empower learners across The Gambia with accessible and innovative educational tools, fostering a culture of lifelong learning, critical thinking, and digital literacy.

02

Vision

Our vision is to revolutionize education in The Gambia by bridging gaps with technology, creating a dynamic and inclusive learning environment where every individual can realize their full potential and contribute positively to society.

03

Aims and Objective

Our institution aims to transform the digital landscape of the Gambia through educating young Gambians in the field of tech and most especially advocating for more women in the field across the country, empowering them to recognize their potential and become leaders. Additionally we offer scholarships to young girls into learning in our institution.
